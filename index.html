We utilise digital tools to mitigate climate change

Green Panthers is an online Youth-Driven Initiative that utilizes Digital tools/Platforms to educate young people on the Effects of Climate Change with keen focus on the Lake Chad region and to also call for Action. Green Panthers also carry out Surveys and Research to collate for Data thus using it to inform Decisions and Policies of Government, Companies, Climate-focused Organizations and Individuals.

Imagination will take us everywhere

Green Panthers was Founded by Abba Ali Yarima Mustapha and Ibrahim Aliyu Izge in January 2019, coming from a Conflict-Ravaged Region with a Desert Encroached Landscape, the Duo set out to change the Narrative considering how the effect of Climate Change have been contributing to the Dynamics of the Conflict across the Region using Tree Planting as an Entry Point.
