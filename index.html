About us

Green Panthers is a Digital Youth-Driven Initiative

that utilizes Digital tools/Platforms to educate young people on the Effects of Climate Change with keen focus on the Lake Chad region and to also call for Action. Green Panthers also carry out Surveys and Research to collate Data thus using it to inform Decisions and Policies of Government, Companies, Climate-focused Organizations and Individuals.

Who we are


Mission

Creating a Network of Young Climate Actors across Lake Chad.

Vision

To see all regional institution are integreted to mitigate climate change.
